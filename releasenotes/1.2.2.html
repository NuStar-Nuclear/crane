What’s New in 1.2.2¶

New Features¶

  1. 增加探测器活度分布的编辑及可视化功能；

  2. 多群常数库更新至RLIB-1.1.0；

  3. 增加从CARIN.OUT中自动读取组件功率分布、探测器活度分布测量值的功能；

Bug Fixes¶

  1. 4e77aec: _crd_cell_index should be updated if core3d rebuilt to avoid crd burnable material not found error

  2. 1181d93: incorrect shape of assem_power and ref_assem_power

  3. 8c36fed: subplane should not be considered when plotting cmfd axial meshes

  4. b8e4a92: vector such as is_thermal_couplingsis_cb_searchings should not be empty

  5. df4856f: heating-local can not tallyed for mgxs cases

  6. 1c159b7: bugs in printing head and iteration info for macro xs cases

  7. e052d78: moderator_dens_old may empty for cases that turn on thermal feedback after no thermal feedback case

  8. 5f9b23a: hdf5 group not close in exportFinalSummaryToHDF5

  9. 127aca2: plane thickness should be multiply for cell_avg_power editing

  10. 02c2088: temperature not correct if not perform thermal feedback and only inlet temperatures provided
