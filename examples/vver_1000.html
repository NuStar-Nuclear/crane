6. X2-VVER-1000¶

相关参考文献如下:

  1. Bilodid, Y., Fridman, E., Lotsch, T., 2020. X2 VVER-1000 benchmark revision: Fresh HZP core state and the reference Monte Carlo solution. Ann. Nucl. Energy 144, 107558.
